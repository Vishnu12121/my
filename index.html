A Whisper of Love

In the quiet glow of the evening sun,

Where whispers of the heart have just begun,

A melody flows, soft and true,

Of dreams that whisper, "I love you."

Like petals adrift on a gentle stream,

Your laughter carries my sweetest dream.

Each glance, a story, deep and bright,

Turning shadows to the purest light.

Will you marry me?

I'm so happy! You said YES!

Maybe next time...
